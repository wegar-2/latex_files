\section{Introduction}

Consider the standard model:
\begin{equation}
Y_t = a x_t + b +\epsilon_t
\end{equation}
where available sample is indexed by $t \in {1, 2, \dots, T}$.
Let ${(x_1, y_1), (x_2, y_2), \dots, (x_T, y_T)}$ be the dataset s.t.:
\begin{equation}
y_t = a x_t + b + e_t
\end{equation}

Minimization of the residual sum of squares:
\begin{equation}
RSS = \sum_{t=1}^{T} e_t^2 = \sum_{t=1}^{T}(y_t - a x_t - b)^2
\end{equation}
